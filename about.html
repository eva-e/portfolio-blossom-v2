Evanthia (Eva) Evgenidis

Multimedia/VisualArtist | Melbourne, Aus

Hello! My name is Evanthia (Eva) Evgenidis and I'm a Multimedia/Visual Artist, passionate about the articulation of poetry through art and design. Presently, I’m in my final year at RMIT, undertaking a Bachelor of Design (Digital Media).

From a young age I have had a passion for expressing poetry through sound, art and design, using a diverse range of visual arts and media forms, such as:


  • 🌸Sound/Music Composition
  • 🌸 Videography/Editing
  • 🌸 Web Development and Design
  • 🌸 Digital Illustrating (Entry-level)
  • 🌸 3D Modelling (Entry-level)
  • 🌸 Photography/Editing
  • 🌸 Painting/Crafts/Mixed Media

    • My journey as a creative practitioner in design began with the production of a variety of design projects which entailed distinct design modes and techniques, including videography, sound composition, film editing, two dimensional graphic design, three dimensional model/environment design and finally website design.

    The literary art of poetry is at the core of my productions, representing my interest and appreciation for classical, poetic and elegant design, embodying my personality.


    As I continue this expedition through art and design, I humbly hope my creative ideas and productions portray my soft and poetic styles and how there is more in less, as the smallest of details can make an immense impact on the expression of emotion, music and nature through the art of design. Working at everything heartily, with love.
